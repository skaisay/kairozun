Photo Editor
How to Use

Welcome to the Kairozun Photo Editor!

  • 🎨 Filter — open the left panel to choose a filter for your photo
  • ✏️ Text — type in the watermark field, press Enter to place it, then type again for more
  • 😀 Stickers — click an emoji to add it to text, or drag it onto the photo
  • 🎨 Color — pick a color for your text watermark
  • 🔤 Font — choose a font for your text
  • 📏 Size — use +/− buttons to change text size
  • ✋ Drag — drag any watermark to reposition it on the photo
  • ❌ Remove — double-click any watermark to delete it
  • 💾 Save — exports the edited photo to your PC
  • ↺ Reset — clears all edits and starts over
  • ✂️ Crop — select an area to crop the photo
  • 💧 Blur — drag over an area to blur/hide sensitive information
